The **FREE** solution to automatically notify users about old unpublished drafts in WordPress. Enhance content productivity and stay organized with Email Draft Notifier.

== Description ==

**Email Draft Notifier** is designed to streamline content management for WordPress website owners, editors, and authors. Our plugin helps you stay on top of old unpublished drafts, ensuring that content doesn't get forgotten or overlooked.

**Key Features**:
* **Automated Notifications**: Receive periodic email reminders about old drafts.
* **Customizable Email Templates**: Craft your messages with a built-in WYSIWYG editor.
* **Flexible Notification Recipients**: Choose to notify administrators, authors, or both.
* **Configurable Threshold**: Set how old a draft needs to be before triggering a notification.
* **Seamless Integration**: Works with all post types, including custom post types.

Whether you're a blogger, business owner, or content manager, Email Draft Notifier keeps you informed and your content strategy on track.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `Email-draft-notifier` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Navigate to the Email Draft Notifier settings page to configure your preferences.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Why do I need a draft notifier? =
To ensure you don't forget about content you've started but haven't published. It's easy to overlook drafts, especially on busy sites. Our plugin keeps you informed and helps manage your content strategy.

= Can I customize the email sent? =
Absolutely! Email Draft Notifier comes with a built-in WYSIWYG editor, allowing you to craft personalized notification emails.

= Does the plugin support custom post types? =
Yes, the plugin works seamlessly with all post types, including custom ones.
